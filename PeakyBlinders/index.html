Peaky Blinders

"May You Be In Heaven A Full Half Hour Before The Devil Knows You're Dead"

About

A gangster family epic, set in the lawless streets of Birmingham post the First World War

Summary

Season 1

Crime pays well. Business pays better.

"Peaky Blinders" is set in 1919 Small Heath, Birmingham, where Thomas Shelby (Cillian Murphy) and his notorious family gang, known for their razor blade caps, dominate the local underworld with illegal activities. When a crate of guns goes missing, Tommy sees an opportunity to expand their influence, sparking conflicts with rival gangs and political factions. With its stellar cast and gritty storytelling, the series redefines period dramas, offering a captivating glimpse into a forgotten chapter of British history.

Season 2

Time to make some real money.

In the roaring 1920s, the Peaky Blinders gang led by crime boss Thomas Shelby expands their criminal operations to London amidst encounters with hedonism, revenge, and covert paramilitary operations, all while navigating personal entanglements and societal power struggles.

Season 3

You can choose what you do, but you can’t choose what you want.

On his wedding day, Thomas Shelby is ensnared by a clandestine organization, propelling him into a complex web of global intrigue and perilous alliances, while facing his most daunting challenges yet and navigating the expansion of his criminal empire across British society and into America.

Season 4

I’m not a traitor to my class. I’m just an extreme example of what a working man can achieve.

In December 1925, estranged from his family and solely focused on business, Tommy Shelby OBE receives a cryptic letter on Christmas Eve, prompting him to realize the imminent danger facing the Shelbys, leading him to flee to Small Heath, Birmingham, the only place he deems safe, as he confronts the threat of annihilation alongside new allies and adversaries.

Season 5

Sometimes, death is a kindness.

Amid the chaos of the 1929 Wall Street Crash, Thomas Shelby MP is faced with a pivotal decision when approached by a charismatic politician, realizing that his response not only affects his family's future but the fate of the entire nation, while grappling with personal demons in his quest to combat a rising dark force threatening Britain's stability and confronting the complexities of morality and redemption.

Season 6

I Have No Limitations.

Thomas Shelby confronts betrayal, impending war, and internal strife within his criminal empire, forcing him to navigate treacherous waters while grappling with the ghosts of his past. As tensions escalate and enemies close in, Tommy must rely on his cunning and resourcefulness to protect his family and maintain control amidst a turbulent landscape of shifting alliances and deadly threats.
